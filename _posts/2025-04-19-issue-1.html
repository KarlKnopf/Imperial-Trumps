Welcome To The Neighborhood! Issue 1



What is this?

Welcome To The Neighborhood! is a weekly newsletter dedicated to informing the public on anything they could possibly want to know about The Intergalactic Society! What is The Intergalactic Society? It is a movement dedicated to bringing the universe together one galaxy at a time! As of now we have over 400,000 member galaxies, with Megaska as it's latest member! Speaking of which, it is Megaska that we are going to wright about in our first issue! This issue will cover the highlights of the galaxy to show current and future extragalactic readers what’s so great about it! Of course I’m speaking of only the highlights, it would be both impossible and impractical to list absolutely everything! So without further adieu, let’s begin!

  Megaska

Megaska, majestically floating through the void!

 

 

Megaska is home to roughly 200 billion star systems, and those star systems are home to countless number of people from all walks of life, and a countless number of species! Megaska goes by many names: humanity calls it The Milky Way, the hengowians know it as The Thing With Many Arms, among countless others, however The official name of the galaxy, due to popular vote on Year 2525, is Megaska; which is Korgolian for "Our Home".

 

 Galaxy Highlights

 First off, we have Lok-Zok-Pon, A planet, it is brown with green patches, not patches of grass, but clouds of nitrogen, carbon monoxide, and carbon dioxide. Below the clouds are large rivers and lakes of liquid nitrogen and carbon monoxide. However in this seemingly uninhabitable world, life thrives! Enter the drelltari, a sapient species of fish. They are highly intelligent, however evolution hasn't been too kind to them as they still need to breath the liquids of their world in order to survive, and as such much of the planet's surface is inaccessible to them, not to mention how living in a liquid medium impacts technological development! Then stepped in The Intergalactic Society. We donated drelltari friendly technology to them, and now they are our peers, exploring the cosmos alongside us! This is the power of intergalactic society, Join Today!

The Planet Lok-Zok-Pon, and two of it's 14 moons!



Next up is some very good news! We recently got word from Megaska security personnel that the fourth arm has been successfully cleared of pirates, now we are safe to trade in that part of the galaxy once again! To any extragalactic readers out there, that means Megaska is officially open for business once again if you would like to trade

Speaking of trade, Zeecha & Lambdarr, delivery company and one of our sponsors, would like to let the extragalactic community know that they successfully acquired ships that are powerful enough to bridge galactic divide, even if you are not a resident of Megaska, their services are still available to you! They were one of the few delivery companies undeterred by the pirate menace of the fourth arm while that crisis was occurring, so their very good at what they do!

The home planet, and base of operations for Zeecha & Lambdarr.


 

Fourthly, we have Yum! CO.. Yum! CO. is also a sponsor and Megaska's top food distributor! They have perfected the galaxy's food technology in such a way that they can synthesize any food out of any ingredients that taste just like the original, a boon for the vegan/vegetarian populations, picky eaters, restricted diets, and for dishes normally made from rare ingredients! So if you're a connoisseur of intergalactic delights, Megaska is a go to galaxy!
